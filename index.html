First-Frame (Keyframe) Mode

Reference image = the exact starting frame (keyframe pulled from the edit). Hold composition, framing, lighting, wardrobe, background precisely. Static camera. Aftermath: the particle cloud drifts and thins over the background. COMPLETENESS: every part dissolves — head, torso, arms, HANDS, fingers. NOTHING remains. Anyone else in frame stays fully solid and unaffected. GOLD variant: warm golden ember flecks — firefly sparks with motion-blur streaks. BLACK variant: predominantly black soot/ash (80–90%) + sparse gold glints (10–20%).
